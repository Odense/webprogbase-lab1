Memo is a free online notebook in your web browser. 
With Memo, you can create notes (ideas, to-do-list / to-do list, 
links, or any other simple text without any formatting)
that you would like to write down simply in a web browser online. 
This simple online notebook has an auto-save function
 and allows you to restore your text "as a draft"
even if you closed your browser / browser window
 or tab (if technologically possible and supported by the browser). 
Thus, you can easily return to your text notes anytime again.
